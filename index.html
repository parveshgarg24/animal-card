Asian red fox

Red foxes are generally solitary animals.These animals are well known for their intelligence and adaptability.

  • Head-Body Length: 45-84 cm
  • Weight: 4-5 kg
  • Habitat: Woodlands, open country
  • Diet: Rodents, rabbits, hares, birds, insects

The breeding season for North American red foxes varies according to the geographic range of the specific population. Gestation lasts 51-63 days, after which four pups are born. Both parents help to care for the young, which are weaned by ten weeks of age.
